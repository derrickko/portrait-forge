# Portrait Generation

Generate a **square 1:1 head-and-shoulders studio portrait** of a ${SUBJECT_TYPE}.

## Subject

- Looking directly at the camera
- Lips sealed together, mouth fully closed
- Close-up framing: head, neck, and upper shoulders only

## Expression

- Expression is determined and match-ready — the look of a player walking onto the pitch

## Art Style

Photojournalistic sports portrait — a professional match-day photograph taken pitchside with a long telephoto lens.

- Raw, unretouched skin: visible pores, peach fuzz, uneven tone, natural redness on cheeks and ears, minor blemishes, under-eye shadows, and any moles or freckles from the reference
- Natural sheen from exertion — perspiration on forehead and temples
- Windswept hair as if mid-match — individual flyaway strands visible
- Eyes sharp with natural catchlight reflections
- Warm golden-hour daylight tones, slightly desaturated greens from the pitch

## Lighting & Camera

- Warm directional sunlight from one side with soft ambient fill
- Shallow depth of field, subject tack-sharp, background blown out to pure white (#FFFFFF)
- 200-400mm telephoto compression
- Gentle chromatic fringing on high-contrast edges, natural vignette

## Outfit

The subject wears a ${OUTFIT_DESCRIPTOR} neckline. Integrate the fabric seamlessly with the body — preserve the main colors and structure from the outfit reference image. The neckline and collar should feel natural and coherent. Visible jersey mesh texture with realistic wrinkles from movement.

## Identity (highest priority)

Identity preservation takes priority over stylization. Match face shape, jawline, eyes, brows, nose, and lip proportions from the reference. Hair must match the reference in color, texture, length, and parting. The result should be indistinguishable from a real photograph of this person.

## Constraints

Do NOT include any of the following:

- Subject shoulders or body touching or near the left, right, or bottom edge of the image
- Visible arms, waist, torso below the chest, or full body
- Illustrated, cartoon, cel-shaded, vector, or outline styles
- Text, watermarks, UI elements, borders, or logos
- Off-center framing, floating head, or mismatched neck tone
- Distorted collar, teeth, or open mouth
