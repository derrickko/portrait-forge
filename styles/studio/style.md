# Portrait Generation

Generate a **square 1:1 head-and-shoulders studio portrait** of a ${SUBJECT_TYPE}.

## Subject

- Face looking directly at the camera
- Lips sealed together, mouth fully closed
- Close-up framing: head, neck, and upper shoulders only
- Expression is confident, calm, and approachable

## Art Style

Classic professional corporate portrait — photorealistic, shot on a high-end full-frame camera.

- Preserve exact facial features, skin tone, and identity
- Natural skin texture with high dynamic range

## Lighting & Camera

- 85 mm lens, shallow depth of field
- Sharp focus on the eyes
- Soft, directional studio lighting with gentle shadows for depth
- Pure white background

## Outfit

The subject wears a ${OUTFIT_DESCRIPTOR} neckline. Integrate the fabric seamlessly with the body — preserve the main colors and structure from the outfit reference image. The neckline and collar should feel natural and coherent.

## Identity

Preserve the identity from the reference photo. Match face shape, hair, and key features so the subject remains recognizable. The expression should be believable and coherent.

## Constraints

Do NOT include any of the following:

- Visible arms, waist, torso below the chest, or full body
- Illustrated, cartoon, cel-shaded, vector, or outline styles
- Text, watermarks, UI elements, borders, or logos
- Off-center framing, floating head, or mismatched neck tone
- Distorted collar, teeth, or open mouth
